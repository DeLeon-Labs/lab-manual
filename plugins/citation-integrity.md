# Citation Integrity

## Charter

Citation Integrity evaluates whether claims and references remain traceable,
well-supported, and accurately represented.

## Owns

- Citation parsing and source linkage
- Claim-to-source inspection
- Integrity findings and uncertainty reporting
- Re-verification when referenced material changes

## Does not own

- Discovering sources for general inspiration
- Deciding the user's argument or creative direction
- Rewriting notes without an explicit delegated action
- Context assembly

## Integration expectations

Citation Integrity consumes claims, citations, and source references from a
context package. Findings must distinguish verified evidence, unresolved issues,
and tool limitations, and must point back to the material inspected.

## Success criteria

Users can see which claims are supported, which need attention, and why a finding
was made.
