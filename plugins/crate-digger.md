# Crate Digger

## Charter

Crate Digger helps users discover, collect, and revisit source material worth
bringing into a creative project.

## Owns

- Source discovery and collection workflows
- Collection metadata and source attribution
- Curation signals used to revisit material
- Export of source references into ecosystem context

## Does not own

- Canonical context assembly
- Claim verification
- Note transformation
- Interpretation of a user's complete knowledge graph

## Integration expectations

Crate Digger supplies durable source references and provenance to Lighthouse and
other authorized receivers. A discovered item remains distinguishable from the
user's own authored material.

## Success criteria

Collected material is easy to rediscover and can enter another workflow without
losing its origin or collection context.
