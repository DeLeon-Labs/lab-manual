# Entropeon

## Charter

Entropeon helps users explore productive uncertainty, variation, and unexpected
connections in creative material.

## Owns

- Entropy-oriented exploration modes
- Controlled variation and recombination
- Explanations of what inputs shaped an exploration
- User controls for novelty and constraint

## Does not own

- Canonical context assembly
- Quietly replacing source material with generated variants
- Verification of factual claims
- General-purpose graph navigation

## Integration expectations

Entropeon consumes bounded context packages and emits clearly labeled candidate
directions with lineage back to the inputs. Outputs are proposals, not edits,
until a user explicitly applies them through the appropriate owner.

## Success criteria

Users encounter useful surprise without losing control of constraints, sources,
or the distinction between original and generated material.
