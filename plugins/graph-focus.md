# Graph Focus

## Charter

Graph Focus helps users navigate a connected body of material by narrowing the
visible graph to the relationships relevant to the task at hand.

## Owns

- Focused graph views and neighborhood navigation
- Filters, paths, and relationship emphasis
- Explanations of why nodes and edges are visible
- Export of user-selected graph context

## Does not own

- The canonical storage of every plugin's data
- Context assembly for all ecosystem workflows
- Mutating source relationships without explicit authority
- Note transformation or claim verification

## Integration expectations

Graph Focus reads authorized nodes, edges, and provenance from ecosystem context.
It returns user selections and paths to Lighthouse without claiming ownership of
the underlying source records.

## Success criteria

Users can move from an overwhelming network to a comprehensible, task-relevant
view while retaining the meaning and provenance of relationships.
