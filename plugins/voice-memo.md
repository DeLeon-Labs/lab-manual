# Voice Memo

## Charter

Voice Memo captures spoken thought and prepares it for deliberate use elsewhere
in the ecosystem.

## Owns

- Audio capture and playback
- Transcript generation and correction workflows
- Time-aligned references between audio and transcript
- Authorized handoff of voice-derived material

## Does not own

- General note transformation
- Ecosystem-wide context assembly
- Treating an imperfect transcript as unquestionably authoritative
- Citation verification

## Integration expectations

Voice Memo exports audio references, transcript segments, timestamps, and
confidence or correction metadata. Lighthouse can include these in context;
Note Actions can transform selected transcript material.

## Success criteria

A spoken idea can move into another workflow while remaining connected to its
audio source and any transcript uncertainty.
